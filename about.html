About Me

Hello! My name is Weston Rose, and I have 7 years of experience working in higher education administration as a communications coordinator, where one of my responsibilities is updating our website using Drupal, an open-source content management system. In November 2019, I received my certificate in Visual Design for web content from General Assembly. In this course, my classmates and I learn to use Adobe XD, practiced wireframing, and covered topics like color theory and typography. In addition updating my office's website, I frequently use the Adobe Creative Suite in conjunction with Microsoft Excel to style and create graphs, logos, one pagers, and conference booklets. Prior to my career as communications specialist, I worked for over two years as an adjunct instructor of Rhetoric & Composition to freshmen college students. I hold bachelor's and master's degrees in English from Sam Houston State University
